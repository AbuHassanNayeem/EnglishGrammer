## 1. Introduction
The **Subject** is the "doer" of the action or the "topic" of the sentence. It is the most critical element for sentence building, as it governs the verb and dictates the logic of the entire pattern.

## 5. Summary of Subject Types

| Category | Example | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Simple** | ***Dogs*** *bark.* | Single word. |
| **Complete** | ***The big dog*** *barks.* | Head + Modifiers. |
| **Verbal** | ***Swimming*** *is fun.* | Gerund/Infinitive. |
| **Clausal** | ***That he lied*** *is sad.* | Full Clause. |
| **Dummy** | ***It*** *is 5 PM.* | "Empty" subject. |
