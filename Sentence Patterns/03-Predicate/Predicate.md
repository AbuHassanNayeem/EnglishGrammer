## 1. Introduction
The **Predicate** is the entire part of the sentence that comments on the Subject. While the Subject is the "topic", the Predicate is the "comment" or "action".
*   *Subject:* **The bird**
*   *Predicate:* **flew away quickly.**

This chapter covers the structural and linguistic properties of predicates.

## 3. Quick Summary

*   **Simple Predicate:** Just the verb (*runs*).
*   **Complete Predicate:** Verb + modifiers (*runs fast in the rain*).
*   **Compound Predicate:** Two verbs (*runs and jumps*).
