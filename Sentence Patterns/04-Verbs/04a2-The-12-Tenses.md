## 1. The Structure of English Time
English combines **3 Times** (Past, Present, Future) with **4 Aspects** (Simple, Continuous, Perfect, Perfect Continuous).

---

## 2. Present Tenses

### Present Simple
*   **Formula:** S + V1 / V-s
*   **Use:** Facts, habits, universal truths.
*   *I work.* / *She works.*

### Present Continuous
*   **Formula:** S + am/is/are + V-ing
*   **Use:** Happening *now*, temporary situations.
*   *I am working.* / *She is working.*

### Present Perfect
*   **Formula:** S + have/has + V3
*   **Use:** Past action with present result, life experience.
*   *I have worked.* / *She has worked.*

### Present Perfect Continuous
*   **Formula:** S + have/has + been + V-ing
*   **Use:** Started in past, continuing now (focus on duration).
*   *I have been working.* / *She has been working.*

---

## 3. Past Tenses

### Past Simple
*   **Formula:** S + V2
*   **Use:** Completed past action at a specific time.
*   *I worked.* / *She worked.*

### Past Continuous
*   **Formula:** S + was/were + V-ing
*   **Use:** Action in progress at a past moment (interrupted).
*   *I was working (when called).*

### Past Perfect
*   **Formula:** S + had + V3
*   **Use:** Action before a past time (Past of the Past).
*   *I had worked (before I stopped).*

### Past Perfect Continuous
*   **Formula:** S + had + been + V-ing
*   **Use:** Continuous action before a past time.
*   *I had been working (for hours when...)*

---

## 4. Future Tenses

### Future Simple
*   **Formula:** S + will + V1
*   **Use:** Prediction, promise, instant decision.
*   *I will work.*

### Future Continuous
*   **Formula:** S + will + be + V-ing
*   **Use:** In progress at a future time.
*   *I will be working (at 5 PM).*

### Future Perfect
*   **Formula:** S + will + have + V3
*   **Use:** Completed by a future time.
*   *I will have worked (by 2025).*

### Future Perfect Continuous
*   **Formula:** S + will + have + been + V-ing
*   **Use:** Duration up to a future point.
*   *I will have been working (for 10 years).*

---

## 5. Practice Exercises

### Identify the Tense
1. She had been sleeping.
2. We will call you.
3. They look happy.
4. He turned off the light.
5. I have noticed a change.

> **Answers:**
> 1. **Past Perfect Continuous**
> 2. **Future Simple**
> 3. **Present Simple**
> 4. **Past Simple**
> 5. **Present Perfect**
