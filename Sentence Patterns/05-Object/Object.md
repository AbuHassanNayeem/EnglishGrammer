## 1. Introduction
The **Object** is a core component of the English sentence predicate. It represents the person or thing that receives action or is acted upon. Unlike the Subject (the doer), the Object is the receiver.

This chapter breaks down the different types of objects and their rules.

## 3. Quick Summary

*   **Direct Object:** Answers "What?" or "Whom?"
*   **Indirect Object:** Answers "To whom?" or "For whom?"
*   **Case:** Objects always use the **Objective Case** (me, him, her, us, them).
*   **Structure:**
    *   S + V + DO (*I eat cake.*)
    *   S + V + IO + DO (*I give him cake.*)
