## 1. Introduction
A **Complement** completes the meaning of a subject or object. Unlike an object (which receives action), a complement describes or renames. It is essential for the sentence to make sense.

This chapter details the types, forms, and identification of complements.

## 3. Quick Summary

*   **Subject Complement:** Follows Linking Verbs (*be, become, seem*). Equation: S = C.
*   **Object Complement:** Follows Direct Objects. Equation: O = C.
*   **Adverbial:** Not a complement. Describes *Time/Place* vs *Identity/Quality*.
    *   *He is **here**.* (Adverbial - Place).
    *   *He is **happy**.* (Complement - Quality).
