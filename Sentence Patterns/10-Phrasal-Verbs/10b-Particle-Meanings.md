## 1. Introduction
Phrasal verbs often seem random, but the particle (Up, Down, Off) often carries a core concept.

---

## 2. UP (Completion / Increase / Upward)
*   **Completion:** *Eat up* (finish eating), *Clean up* (clean completely), *Use up* (deplete).
    *   *Drink up! It's time to go.*
*   **Increase:** *Turn up* (volume), *Speak up* (louder), *Speed up*.
*   **Appearance:** *Show up* (appear), *Turn up* (arrive).
*   **Creation:** *Make up* (invent), *Set up* (establish), *Dream up*.

---

## 3. DOWN (Decrease / Failure / Record)
*   **Decrease:** *Turn down* (volume), *Calm down*, *Slow down*.
*   **Failure/Stop:** *Break down* (machine stops), *Shut down* (close).
*   **Recording:** *Write down*, *Take down*, *Note down*.

---

## 4. OFF (Separation / Departure / Cancellation)
*   **Separation:** *Cut off*, *Take off* (clothes/plane).
*   **Departure:** *Set off* (start journey), *Drive off*.
*   **Cancellation:** *Call off* (cancel), *Put off* (postpone).
*   **Deactivation:** *Turn off*, *Switch off*, *Log off*.

---

## 5. ON (Continuation / Attachment / Activation)
*   **Continuation:** *Go on* (continue), *Keep on*, *Drive on*.
*   **Attachment:** *Put on* (clothes), *Try on*.
*   **Activation:** *Turn on*, *Switch on*, *Log on*.

---

## 6. OUT (Exit / Discovery / Exhaustion)
*   **Exit:** *Go out*, *Take out*, *Kick out*.
*   **Discovery:** *Find out*, *Figure out*, *Point out*.
*   **Exhaustion:** *Run out* (deplete), *Burn out*, *Sell out*.

---

## 7. Practice Exercises

### Match the Meaning (Completion vs Direction):
1. He walked **up** the hill.
2. He ate **up** the burger.

> **Answers:**
> 1. **Direction** (Literal movement).
> 2. **Completion** (Finished it).
