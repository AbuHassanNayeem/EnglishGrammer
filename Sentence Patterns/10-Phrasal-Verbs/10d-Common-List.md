## 1. Work & Business
*   **Call off:** Cancel. (*They called off the meeting.*)
*   **Put off:** Postpone. (*Let's put off the discussion.*)
*   **Fill out:** Complete a form. (*Fill out this application.*)
*   **Take over:** Take control. (*He took over the project.*)
*   **Draw up:** Draft/Write. (*Draw up a contract.*)
*   **Burn out:** Work too hard. (*Employees are burning out.*)
*   **Lay off:** Fire staff. (*They laid off 50 workers.*)

## 2. Travel & Movement
*   **Check in:** Register. (*Check in at the hotel.*)
*   **Check out:** Leave/Pay. (*Check out by 11 AM.*)
*   **Take off:** Plane departs. (*We took off on time.*)
*   **Get in:** Arrive (train/plane). (*When does the train get in?*)
*   **Drop off:** Transport someone. (*Can you drop me off?*)
*   **Pick up:** Collect someone. (*I will pick you up.*)
*   **Set off:** Start journey. (*We set off at dawn.*)

## 3. Communication
*   **Bring up:** Mention. (*Don't bring up that topic.*)
*   **Point out:** Highlight. (*She pointed out the error.*)
*   **Break in:** Interrupt. (*Sorry to break in, but...*)
*   **Talk over:** Discuss. (*Let's talk over the plan.*)
*   **Call back:** Return a call. (*I'll call you back.*)

## 4. Daily Life
*   **Wake up:** Stop sleeping.
*   **Get up:** Leave bed.
*   **Put on:** Wear.
*   **Take off:** Remove clothes.
*   **Clean up:** Tidy.
*   **Throw away:** Discard.
*   **Look for:** Search.
