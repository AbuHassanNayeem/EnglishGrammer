## 1. Introduction
A **Phrasal Verb** is a multi-word verb consisting of a Main Verb + Particle (Preposition/Adverb).
*   *Meaning:* Idiomatic (cannot be guessed from individual words).
*   *Structure:* Often behaves differently than normal verbs regarding object placement.

This chapter breaks down the structure, meaning, and usage of phrasal verbs.

## 3. Quick Summary

*   **Separable:** You can move the object (*Turn **it** on*).
*   **Inseparable:** You cannot move the object (*Run into **him***).
*   **Pronoun Rule:** If the object is a pronoun, it MUST separate a separable phrasal verb.
