## 1. Introduction
**Subject-Verb Agreement** is the rule that the Verb must match the Subject in Number (Singular vs Plural).
*   *The boy runs.* (Singular).
*   *The boys run.* (Plural).

While simple in principle, complex sentences involving intervening phrases, compounds, and indefinite pronouns often cause errors. This chapter covers all agreement rules.

## 3. Quick Tips
*   **Prepositional Phrases:** Cross them out! *The box ~~of books~~ is heavy.*
*   **Or/Nor:** Look at the noun closest to the verb.
*   **Each/Every:** Always singular.
