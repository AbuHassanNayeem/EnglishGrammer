## 1. Definition
**Modifiers** are words, phrases, or clauses that add extra information to a sentence. They describe the subject, object, or verb.
*   **Key Distinction:** **Pre-modifiers** come before importance words; **Post-modifiers** come after.

---

## 2. Adjectives (Modifying Nouns)

### A. Attributive Position (Before Noun)
*   *Structure:* [Determiner] + [Adjective] + Noun.
*   *Example:* *The **red** car is fast.*

**Order of Adjectives:**
When multiple adjectives are used, follow this order:
1.  **Opinion:** *Beautiful*
2.  **Size:** *Large*
3.  **Age:** *Old*
4.  **Shape:** *Round*
5.  **Color:** *Red*
6.  **Origin:** *Italian*
7.  **Material:** *Wooden*
8.  **Purpose:** *Dining*
*   *Example:* *A beautiful large old round Italian wooden dining table.*

### B. Predicative Position (After Linking Verb)
*   *Structure:* Subject + Linking Verb + Adjective.
*   *Example:* *The car is **red**.*

---

## 3. Adverbs (Modifying Verbs/Adjs/Adverbs)

### A. Modifying Verbs
*   **Frequency:** Usually before main verb. (*She **always** arrives.*)
*   **Manner/Place/Time:** Usually after verb. (*She runs **quickly**.*)

### B. Modifying Adjectives/Adverbs
*   Always before the word.
    *   *She is **very** tall.*
    *   *He runs **very** quickly.*

### C. Modifying Sentences
*   Often at the beginning.
    *   ***Unfortunately**, we missed the train.*

---

## 4. General Modifiers (Pre vs Post)

### A. Pre-modifiers
*   *The **beautifully decorated** room.* (Adverb+Adj before Noun).

### B. Post-modifiers
*   *The room **decorated beautifully**.* (Participle phrase after Noun).
*   *The man **who is wearing a hat**.* (Relative clause after Noun).

---

## 5. Summary Table

| Modifier Type | Typical Position | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Adjective** | Before noun / After link verb | *Red car* / *Car is red* |
| **Adverb (verb)** | Before or after verb | *She quickly ran* / *She ran quickly* |
| **Adverb (adj)** | Before adjective | *Very happy* |
| **Adverb (adv)** | Before adverb | *Very quickly* |
| **Adverbial Phrase** | Begin / Mid / End | *After lunch, we walked.* |
| **General** | Pre or Post | *Decorated room* / *Room decorated* |
