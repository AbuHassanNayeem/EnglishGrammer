## 1. Wish (Regrets & Unreal Desires)

### Wish + Past Simple (Present Regret)
Expresses dissatisfaction with the **present** situation.

**Structure:** Wish + Past Simple / were

**Examples:**
- *I **wish** I **were** taller.* (I'm not tall - I want to be)
- *She **wishes** she **had** more time.* (She doesn't have time)
- *I **wish** I **could** speak French.* (I can't speak French)

### Wish + Past Perfect (Past Regret)  
Expresses regret about the **past** (cannot change).

**Structure:** Wish + Past Perfect

**Examples:**
- *I **wish** I **had studied** harder.* (I didn't study - now I regret it)
- *She **wishes** she **had gone** to the party.* (She didn't go - she regrets it)
- *I **wish** I **hadn't said** that.* (I said it - I regret it)

### Wish + would (Annoyance/Request for Change)
Expresses **annoyance** about someone's behavior or a request for change.

**Examples:**
- *I **wish** you **would listen** to me!* (You don't listen - it annoys me)
- *I **wish** it **would stop** raining.* (The rain continues - I want it to stop)

**Note:** NOT used with "I wish I would..." (you can't be annoyed with yourself).

## 2. If Only (Stronger Wish - Regret/Desire)

**If only** = Wish (but more emphatic/emotional)

### Same Structures
- **Present:** *If only I **were** rich!*
- **Past:** *If only I **had known**!*
- **Would:** *If only you **would** help me!*

---

## 3. Hope (Real Possibility)

### Hope vs Wish
- **Hope:** Possible/likely future event
- **Wish:** Unreal/unlikely/regretful

**Hope Structure:** Hope + Present/Future

**Examples:**
- *I **hope** (that) it **doesn't rain** tomorrow.* (It's possible)
- *I **hope** (that) you **will pass**.* (Realistic expectation)

**Compare:**
- *I **hope** I pass the exam.* (I think I might! - real)
- *I **wish** I passed the exam.* (I failed/doubtful - unreal)

## 4. Summary Table

| Expression | Time | Structure | Meaning | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Wish** | Present | Past Simple | Unreal now | *I wish I were rich.* |
| **Wish** | Past | Past Perfect | Regret past | *I wish I had studied.* |
| **Wish** | Behavior | would + base | Annoyance | *I wish you would listen!* |
| **If only** | Any | Same as wish | Stronger wish | *If only I had known!* |
| **Hope** | Future | Present/will | Real possibility | *I hope you pass.* |

## 5. Practice Exercises

### Choose wish or hope and the correct form:
1. I ______ I ______ (be) taller. (unreal present)
2. I ______ you ______ (come) to the party tomorrow. (real future)
3. I ______ I ______ (study) harder last year. (past regret)

> **Answers:**
> 1. wish, were (present unreal)
> 2. hope, will come / come (future real)
> 3. wish, had studied (past regret)
