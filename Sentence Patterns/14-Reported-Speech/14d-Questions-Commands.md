# Reporting Questions & Commands — Detailed Guide

## 1. Reporting Yes/No Questions

### Structure
**ask + if/whether + statement word order**

### Key Changes
1. **Remove question mark**
2. **Use if/whether**
3. **Change to statement word order** (Subject + Verb)
4. **Backshift tenses**

### Examples

| Direct Question | Reported (Syntax Focus) |
|:---|:---|
| *"**Are you** happy?"* | *He asked **if I was** happy.* |
| *"**Do you** like it?"* | *She asked **if I liked** it.* |
| *"**Can you** swim?"* | *He asked **if I could** swim.* |
| *"**Have you** finished?"* | *She asked **if I had** finished.* |

### If vs Whether
Both are correct, but **whether** is more formal:
- *He asked **if** I was ready.* (common)
- *He asked **whether** I was ready.* (formal)

---

## 2. Reporting Wh- Questions

### Structure
**ask + question word + statement word order**

### Examples

| Direct Question | Reported (Syntax Focus) |
|:---|:---|
| *"**Where do you** live?"* | *He asked **where I lived**.* |
| *"**What are you** doing?"* | *She asked **what I was** doing.* |
| *"**When will you** come?"* | *He asked **when I would** come.* |
| *"**Why did you** leave?"* | *She asked **why I had** left.* |

### Who/What as Subject (No Change)
When Who/What is the subject, word order stays:
- *"**Who broke** the window?" → He asked **who had broken** the window.*
- *"**What happened**?" → She asked **what had happened**.*

---

## 3. Reporting Commands & Requests

### Structure
**tell/ask/order + person + (not) to + infinitive**

### Commands (Tell/Order)
- *"**Sit down**!" → He **told me to** sit down.*
- *"**Don't** move!" → She **told me not to** move.*
- *"**Be** quiet!" → He **ordered us to** be quiet.*

### Requests (Ask)
- *"Please **help** me." → She **asked me to** help her.*
- *"Please **don't** tell anyone." → He **asked me not to** tell anyone.*

### Suggestions/Advice
- *"**Let's** go home." → She **suggested** going home.*
- *"You **should** study." → He **advised me to** study.*

---

## 4. Summary Table

| Type | Direct | Reported Structure |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Yes/No Q** | *"Are you ready?"* | *ask + **if/whether** + statement order* |
| **Wh- Q** | *"Where do you live?"* | *ask + **wh-word** + statement order* |
| **Command** | *"Sit down!"* | *tell/order + person + **to** + infinitive* |
| **Request** | *"Please help me."* | *ask + person + **to** + infinitive* |
| **Negative** | *"Don't go!"* | *tell + person + **not to** + infinitive* |

---

## 5. Common Mistakes

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|:---|:---|
| *He asked **if was I** ready.* | *He asked **if I was** ready.* |
| *She asked **where do I** live.* | *She asked **where I lived**.* |
| *He told **to sit down**.* | *He told **me to** sit down.* |

---

## 6. Practice Exercises

### Convert to reported speech:
1. "Are you coming?" → She asked...
2. "Where is the station?" → He asked...
3. "Close the door!" → She told...

> **Answers:**
> 1. She asked if/whether I was coming.
> 2. He asked where the station was.
> 3. She told me to close the door.
