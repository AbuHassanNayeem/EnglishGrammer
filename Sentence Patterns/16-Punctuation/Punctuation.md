## 3. Quick Reference

### Joining Independent Clauses

| Method | Example |
|:---|:---|
| **Period** | *I ran. She walked.* |
| **Semicolon** | *I ran; she walked.* |
| **Comma + FANBOYS** | *I ran, and she walked.* |
| **Semicolon + Connector** | *I ran; however, she walked.* |

### Common Errors

| Error | Example | Fix |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Comma Splice** | *I ran, she walked.* | Add conjunction or use semicolon |
| **Run-On** | *I ran she walked.* | Add punctuation |
| **Fragment** | *Because I was tired.* | Complete the thought |
